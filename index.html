About Me

Hi! I'm Eka Angelia Yustina, I am a recent graduate from the State Polytechnic of Madiun with a Diploma in Computer Control Engineering. I have an interest in the Internet of Things (IoT), programming, and user interface design. I have completed an internship at PT Industri Kereta Api (INKA) Persero, which provided me with hands-on experience in the industrial sector, particularly in the application of technology and problem-solving in a real-world work environment. I am capable of working independently or as part of a team with strong communication skills, and I have a high level of enthusiasm for continuous learning to further develop my knowledge and skills in the field of technology and innovation. Outside of my professional interests, I enjoy exploring new technologies, reading about the latest trends in tech, and engaging in creative projects that allow me to express myself.
